Sessions

Throughout sessions, our goal is to establish a client relationship. It is ideal to identify your goals as a client, and the goals throughout the life coaching process.

Sessions are both provided in a one-on-one or group setting. It all depends on what your needs are.

SESSIONS

Group Sessions

Baeza Life Coaching understands that sometimes coaching can be done for more than one person at a time. We can assist your group in finding their greatness, developing their leadership and working together as a team.

Take a look at the following to see if your group qualifies for group sessions.

Qualifying Groups

- Corporate Company Trainings- Student Clubs and Organizations

- Student Clubs and Organizations

- Career Fair Activities

-Corporate and Academic Workshops

**Please note that in order to request a group session, there must be a minimum of 10 attendees and we have a maximum of 22 attendees, if more than 22, can be split into different sessions.**

Individual Sessions

Through one-on-one sessions, we will focus directly on meeting the needs of the client. Whether that be gaining confidence, finding a redirection in life, or looking to be a better lead.

After the initial individual session, we will meet the needs of the client to continue to meet weekly, biweekly or when meeting is needed.

We will personalize ever session to the client and work directly towards your goals.

Requirements

- Must be 18+ years of age

- Ability to meet at a minimum biweekly after initial session

- Open-minded and ready for change

- Access to a quiet and calm space for full focus

**Individual sessions can be held in-person or remotely.**

CLARITY COACHING SESSION

What is a clarity coaching session?

At Baeza Life Coaching, we understand how making any choice can have a great impact on your life. That is why we offer our first session for free, then as the client, YOU make the decision if you are ready to commit.

A clarity coaching session is set up to find what aspects of your life you would like to better or change and see if our coaching services are right for you! This is where you can as questions and let us know as much about yourself, such as: What you want? What you are looking to accomplish? What you would like to change or implement to your life?

Find clarity through your first session before committing.
