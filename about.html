About Baeza Life Coaching

Baeza Life Coaching is built and based on having an outlook on life as mountains. Mountains can symbolize the struggles we would like to overcome and get across. The peak of a mountain can also be the goal that we would like to reach.

Baeza Life Coaching is here to help you overcome your fears and assists in reaching your goals.

ABOUT THE COACH

Who is Abraham Baeza?

Abraham is the founder and CEO of Baeza Coaching. He grew up in a trailer park located in Rialto, California. Growing up in an underserved neighborhood, Abraham involved himself in sports and other activities to not fall into what was common in his neighborhood, such as doing drugs or joining a gang.

Later in the future, he acquired a bachelor’s degree in Communications Studies and a master’s in Educational Leadership. Since 2015 Abraham has been working with at-risk individuals, foster youth, and formally incarcerated populations. He is currently the EOP Outreach Specialist at CSUN and is sharing the opportunity that impacted his life to others growing up in similar communities.
